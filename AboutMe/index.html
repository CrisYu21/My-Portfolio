About Me

I am Cris, a first-year computer engineering student. As an introvert, I often avoid socializing with many people. Ever since I was a kid, I’ve been fascinated by the workings of modern engineering marvels — from computers to cars — that make our lives easier today. At home, my Lola was strict, and I was not allowed to play outside the compound. Instead, I stayed indoors, spending hours disassembling and tweaking things, exploring what was inside them, and putting them back together. I built my own toys and spent so much time working that I only went out when it was time to eat. It was my comfort zone, doing things at home instead of meeting people outside.

My interests lie in technology, specifically the military and defense industry. As a child, I watched documentaries and movies about technological innovations in these fields, which inspired my love for aviation. I dream of becoming a pilot in the air force someday.

To fulfill this dream, I chose to pursue a computer engineering degree. This course serves as my stepping stone, not only for flying but also for contributing to science and technology. I aspire to create inventions that benefit not only the scientific community but also industries like military defense. I want to help innovate and push our country towards a brighter future.
